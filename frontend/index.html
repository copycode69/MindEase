About MindEase Companion

Our Mission

MindEase Companion was founded in 2023 with a simple goal: to make mental health support accessible to everyone, anytime, anywhere.

We combine cutting-edge artificial intelligence with proven therapeutic techniques to create a compassionate, always-available companion for your mental wellbeing.

Our team of psychologists, AI experts, and designers work together to ensure MindEase provides safe, helpful support while respecting your privacy.
